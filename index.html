AVERAGE JOES VOTING

Brought to you by The Average Joes

Want to hold an election without having to trust a central authority? Want to have transparency, anonymity, and integrity in your election? Then this is the right place for you.

Welcome to our decentralised voting system, which uses blockchain technology to ensure a fair and transparent process.

This system uses ethereum an open-source, public, blockchain-based distributed computing platform. (Need I say more?) To connect to the ethereum network we recommend using the browser plugin MetaMask.

Click on start election.
There you can set the question, how long everyone has to register, and to vote. You can chose to do basic voting
i.e. pick one candidate out of a list, or preferential voting,
i.e. pick a first and second preferences, and preferences are distributed until one candidate has the majority of votes.


Happy Voting!
